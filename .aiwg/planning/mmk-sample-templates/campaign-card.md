# Campaign Card

## Metadata

- **ID**: `CAM-{project}-{number}` (e.g., CAM-LAUNCH-001)
- **Type**: Operations Card (Campaign)
- **Status**: Draft | Approved | In Progress | Launched | Complete | On Hold | Cancelled
- **Owner**: Campaign Manager / Marketing Lead
- **Contributors**: Content Creator, Designer, Media Buyer
- **Reviewers**: Marketing Director, Brand Manager
- **Team**: Marketing Operations
- **Stakeholders**: List key stakeholders (PR, Sales, Product)
- **Created**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Last Updated**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Launch Date**: YYYY-MM-DD (when campaign goes live)
- **End Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Priority**: Critical | High | Medium | Low
- **Budget**: ${amount} (allocated budget)
- **Related**:

## Definition of Ready

This campaign card is ready for execution when:

- [ ] Campaign strategy approved (STRAT-{id})
- [ ] Budget allocated and approved
- [ ] Target audience and messaging defined
- [ ] Creative assets scheduled or in production
- [ ] Channels and tactics identified
- [ ] KPIs and success metrics established
- [ ] Risk mitigation plan in place
- [ ] All required approvals obtained

## Definition of Done

This campaign is complete when:

- [ ] Campaign end date reached
- [ ] All creative assets delivered and archived
- [ ] Final performance metrics collected
- [ ] Post-campaign report delivered
- [ ] Budget reconciled (spent vs. allocated)
- [ ] Lessons learned documented
- [ ] Lead handoff to Sales complete (if applicable)
- [ ] Campaign assets archived in DAM system

## Agent Notes

### For Campaign Manager

- Track campaign progress daily during active phase
- Escalate budget overruns or performance issues immediately
- Coordinate cross-functional teams (creative, media, analytics)
- Ensure messaging consistency across all channels
- Document decisions and changes in Conversation Log

### For Content Creator

- Refer to Creative Brief (BRIEF-{id}) for asset requirements
- Follow Messaging Matrix (MSG-{id}) for tone and messaging
- Submit assets for review at least {X} days before needed
- Ensure all assets meet brand guidelines
- Track asset status in Asset Inventory table

### For Media Buyer

- Monitor campaign performance against KPIs
- Optimize spend allocation based on channel performance
- Flag underperforming channels for review
- Ensure tracking pixels and UTM parameters are correct
- Provide weekly performance snapshots

### For Analytics Lead

- Set up tracking and dashboards before launch
- Monitor real-time performance during campaign
- Provide weekly performance reports
- Identify optimization opportunities
- Deliver final campaign ROI analysis
