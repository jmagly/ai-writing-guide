# Press Release Template

## Purpose

Provide a standard format for press releases that announce company news, product launches, partnerships, or other newsworthy events. This template ensures consistency, professionalism, and compliance with journalistic standards.

## Ownership & Collaboration

- **Document Owner**: PR Manager / Communications Lead
- **Contributor Roles**: Content Writer, Product Marketing, Executive Communications
- **Reviewers**: Marketing Director, Legal/Compliance, Executive Leadership
- **Automation Inputs**: News summary, key facts, quotes, company background
- **Automation Outputs**: `press-release-{topic}-{date}.md` formatted for distribution

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Headline is newsworthy and compelling (under 80 characters)
- [ ] Dateline includes city, state, and date
- [ ] Lead paragraph answers who, what, when, where, why
- [ ] Body provides context, details, and supporting information
- [ ] Quote(s) from executive or spokesperson included
- [ ] Boilerplate company description current and accurate
- [ ] Contact information complete and correct
- [ ] Legal/compliance review completed
- [ ] Final approval from authorized spokesperson

## Press Release Formatting Guidelines

### Length
- Optimal: 300-500 words
- Maximum: 800 words
- One page is ideal (if printing)

### Structure
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Single idea per paragraph
- Inverted pyramid (most important first)

### Style
- Third person (not first person)
- Active voice
- Present or past tense (not future)
- Avoid exclamation points
- Minimize adjectives and superlatives

### Technical
- Double-space between paragraphs (if printing)
- Use standard fonts (Times New Roman, Arial)
- Include page numbers if multi-page
- Center ### at end of release
- Use ### to indicate end

### SEO Optimization (for online distribution)
- Include target keywords naturally in headline and first paragraph
- Use descriptive alt text for images
- Include relevant links to company website
- Optimize meta description if publishing on company site

## Agent Notes

### For PR Manager

- Assess newsworthiness before issuing press release (not all news warrants one)
- Consider journalist perspective: why should they care?
- Avoid marketing language; maintain journalistic tone
- Time release strategically (avoid Fridays, holidays, competitor events)
- Prepare spokesperson for potential media inquiries
- Track media pickup and adjust future releases based on results

### For Content Writer

- Lead with news, not company background
- Keep sentences short and punchy (under 25 words)
- Use specific facts and data instead of vague claims
- Avoid clichés: "game-changing," "revolutionary," "cutting-edge"
- Make quotes sound human, not corporate
- Read aloud to check flow and clarity

### For Product Marketing

- Focus on customer benefits, not just features
- Provide concrete examples and use cases
- Quantify impact with specific metrics
- Position against market context, not just "we're great"
- Validate technical accuracy of product descriptions

### For Legal/Compliance

- Review all claims for substantiation
- Ensure regulatory compliance (health claims, financial projections, etc.)
- Verify trademark usage and attributions
- Check competitive claims for accuracy
- Approve forward-looking statements (public companies)

### For Executive Communications

- Ensure executive quote aligns with company messaging
- Review tone and positioning for brand consistency
- Coordinate timing with other announcements or events
- Prepare talking points for follow-up media inquiries
- Consider social media amplification strategy

**Acme Corporation Launches Widget AI, First AI-Powered Scheduling Platform for Healthcare**

**New platform reduces administrative burden by 60% while improving patient satisfaction**

---

**SAN FRANCISCO** – November 28, 2025 – Acme Corporation, a leader in B2B SaaS solutions, today announced the launch of Widget AI, the first artificial intelligence platform designed specifically for healthcare provider scheduling. Widget AI automates patient scheduling, reducing administrative burden by 60% while improving patient satisfaction and appointment utilization.

Healthcare providers face increasing administrative burdens that detract from patient care. On average, medical staff spend 16 hours per week on scheduling tasks alone, according to the American Medical Association. Widget AI addresses this challenge by leveraging machine learning to predict optimal appointment times, automate confirmations, and intelligently manage cancellations and rescheduling.

Widget AI integrates seamlessly with existing electronic health record systems and offers three core capabilities: predictive scheduling that reduces no-shows by 40%, automated patient communication via text and email, and real-time capacity optimization. Early adopters have reported a 60% reduction in scheduling time and a 25% increase in appointment utilization.

"Healthcare providers shouldn't have to choose between patient care and administrative efficiency," said Jane Smith, CEO of Acme Corporation. "Widget AI allows them to do both, freeing up valuable time to focus on what matters most—their patients. We've seen remarkable results in our pilot programs, and we're excited to make this available to healthcare providers of all sizes."

Widget AI is available immediately for healthcare providers. Pricing starts at $499 per month for small practices, with enterprise pricing available for health systems. The platform is HIPAA-compliant and has earned SOC 2 Type II certification. Acme has partnered with Epic Systems and Cerner to ensure seamless integration with the two largest EHR platforms in North America.

"Since implementing Widget AI three months ago, our scheduling staff has been able to focus on patient support rather than phone tag," said Dr. Sarah Johnson, Chief Medical Officer at Metro Health Clinic. "Our no-show rate has dropped from 18% to 9%, which has a direct impact on our revenue and our ability to serve more patients."
