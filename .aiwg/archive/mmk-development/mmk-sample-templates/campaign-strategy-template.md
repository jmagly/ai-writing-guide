# Campaign Strategy Template

## Purpose

Define the comprehensive strategy for a marketing campaign including objectives, target audience, messaging, channels, timeline, and success criteria. This document guides campaign planning and execution across all teams.

## Ownership & Collaboration

- **Document Owner**: Marketing Strategist / Campaign Manager
- **Contributor Roles**: Brand Manager, Content Strategist, Media Planner, Creative Director
- **Reviewers**: Marketing Director, Product Marketing, Sales Leadership
- **Automation Inputs**: Business objectives, audience research, competitive analysis, budget parameters
- **Automation Outputs**: `campaign-strategy-{campaign-name}.md` with completed strategy and execution plan

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Executive summary complete with clear campaign rationale
- [ ] Objectives aligned with business goals and measurable
- [ ] Target audience thoroughly researched and documented
- [ ] Messaging framework developed and differentiated
- [ ] Channel strategy defined with rationale
- [ ] Timeline and milestones established
- [ ] Budget allocated across tactics
- [ ] Success metrics and measurement plan defined
- [ ] Risks identified and mitigation strategies outlined
- [ ] Stakeholder approval obtained

## Agent Notes

### For Marketing Strategist

- Ground strategy in business objectives, not marketing vanity metrics
- Validate audience insights with research, not assumptions
- Ensure channel mix reflects where audience actually engages
- Build in flexibility for mid-campaign optimization
- Get stakeholder alignment before detailed execution planning

### For Campaign Manager

- Translate strategy into detailed tactical plans
- Assign clear ownership for every deliverable
- Build realistic timelines with buffer for reviews
- Monitor budget closely, flag overruns early
- Coordinate cross-functional teams to avoid silos

### For Creative Director

- Ensure creative concept supports strategic objectives
- Balance brand consistency with campaign innovation
- Consider production feasibility and budget constraints
- Plan for creative testing before full rollout
- Maintain brand guidelines while allowing creative expression

### For Media Planner

- Justify channel selection with audience data, not hunches
- Model expected performance based on historical benchmarks
- Build in budget for testing and optimization
- Negotiate media buys to maximize efficiency
- Plan for cross-channel attribution complexity

### For Analytics Lead

- Set up tracking infrastructure before campaign launch
- Define measurement framework aligned with objectives
- Plan for real-time monitoring during active campaign
- Identify early warning indicators for underperformance
- Prepare post-campaign ROI analysis template upfront
