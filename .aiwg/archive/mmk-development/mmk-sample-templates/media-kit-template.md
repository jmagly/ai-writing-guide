# Media Kit Template

## Purpose

Provide a comprehensive package of company information, assets, and resources to media, journalists, partners, and other stakeholders. A well-organized media kit makes it easy for external audiences to accurately represent your company, products, and leadership.

## Ownership & Collaboration

- **Document Owner**: PR Manager / Communications Lead
- **Contributor Roles**: Content Writer, Brand Manager, Product Marketing, Executive Communications
- **Reviewers**: Marketing Director, Legal/Compliance, Executive Leadership
- **Automation Inputs**: Company background, leadership bios, product descriptions, brand assets, press releases
- **Automation Outputs**: `media-kit-{company-name}-{version}.md` with complete materials and asset links

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Company overview current and accurate
- [ ] Executive bios and headshots included
- [ ] Product/service descriptions up-to-date
- [ ] Recent press releases and news included
- [ ] High-resolution logos and brand assets available
- [ ] Fact sheet with key statistics current
- [ ] Media contact information correct
- [ ] All links functional and assets accessible
- [ ] Legal review completed for claims and trademarks
- [ ] Hosted on accessible, professional platform

## Agent Notes

### For PR Manager

- Update media kit quarterly or after major news (funding, product launch, leadership changes)
- Monitor which assets are most downloaded to understand media needs
- Keep executive bios current (update at least annually)
- Ensure all links are functional before distributing
- Track media kit usage to identify high-interest areas
- Make assets easily discoverable and downloadable (no forms or barriers)

### For Content Writer

- Keep language clear, factual, and free of marketing hyperbole
- Use active voice and specific metrics
- Maintain consistent tone across all sections
- Update statistics and facts regularly (flag outdated data)
- Ensure all product descriptions align with current positioning

### For Brand Manager

- Verify all brand assets follow current brand guidelines
- Provide multiple file formats for logos (PNG, SVG, EPS)
- Include usage guidelines to prevent brand misuse
- Ensure color codes are accurate across RGB, CMYK, and HEX
- Provide both light and dark background logo variations

### For Legal/Compliance

- Review all claims for accuracy and substantiation
- Ensure trademark attributions are correct
- Verify customer testimonials have written permission
- Check that forward-looking statements have appropriate disclaimers
- Approve usage rights and licensing terms

### For Executive Communications

- Keep executive bios professional but personable
- Highlight relevant expertise and achievements
- Update bios when roles or responsibilities change
- Ensure headshots are high-quality and professional
- Prepare executives for potential media inquiries
