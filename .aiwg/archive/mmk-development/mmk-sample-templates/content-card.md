## Agent Notes

### For Content Creator

- Refer to creative brief (BRIEF-{id}) for detailed direction
- Follow messaging matrix (MSG-{id}) for tone and key messages
- Submit drafts on time to allow for adequate review cycles
- Incorporate feedback constructively; ask clarifying questions
- Track actual time spent vs. budgeted for future estimates

### For Editor/Reviewer

- Provide specific, actionable feedback tied to objectives
- Differentiate between subjective preferences and strategic concerns
- Review for clarity, accuracy, brand alignment, and audience fit
- Complete reviews on schedule to avoid delaying publication
- Praise what's working, not just what needs improvement

### For Brand Manager

- Verify compliance with brand guidelines
- Flag inconsistencies early to minimize rework
- Balance consistency with creative expression
- Provide examples of preferred approaches
- Approve quickly when guidelines are met

### For Campaign Manager

- Ensure content aligns with campaign strategy
- Monitor content production progress
- Coordinate cross-functional reviews efficiently
- Track performance against success metrics
- Identify repurposing opportunities

### For Legal/Compliance

- Review claims for substantiation
- Flag regulatory risks early
- Provide alternative language when rejecting claims
- Educate content team on compliance requirements
- Approve expeditiously to avoid publication delays
