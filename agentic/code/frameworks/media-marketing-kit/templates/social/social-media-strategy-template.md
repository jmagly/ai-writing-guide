## Crisis Protocol

### Severity Levels

| Level | Definition | Response |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Low | Isolated complaint | Community manager handles |
| Medium | Viral negative | Marketing team escalation |
| High | Brand threat | Executive involvement |
| Critical | Legal/safety | Crisis team activation |

### Response Framework

1. **Pause**: Stop scheduled posts
2. **Assess**: Evaluate situation scope
3. **Escalate**: Notify appropriate stakeholders
4. **Respond**: Craft approved response
5. **Monitor**: Track sentiment changes
6. **Report**: Document lessons learned
