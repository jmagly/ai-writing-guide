## Community Standards

### Be Respectful

- Treat all community members with courtesy and respect
- Engage in constructive dialogue, even when you disagree
- Avoid personal attacks, name-calling, or harassment
- Respect diverse perspectives, backgrounds, and experiences

### Be Authentic

- Use your real identity when possible
- Share genuine opinions and experiences
- Don't impersonate others or create fake accounts
- Disclose any professional relationships with {Brand Name}

### Be Constructive

- Contribute value to conversations
- Share relevant, helpful information
- Ask questions thoughtfully
- Provide feedback with the intention to help

### Stay On Topic

- Keep discussions relevant to the community focus
- Use appropriate channels for different topics
- Avoid excessive self-promotion
- Respect the purpose of each community space

## Prohibited Content & Behavior

### We Do Not Allow

**Harmful content**:
- Hate speech, discrimination, or bigotry
- Threats, harassment, or bullying
- Violent or graphic content
- Sexual or adult content (unless platform-appropriate)

**Deceptive practices**:
- Misinformation or deliberately false claims
- Spam, scams, or phishing attempts
- Impersonation or identity theft
- Manipulation of engagement metrics

**Illegal activity**:
- Promotion of illegal products or services
- Intellectual property infringement
- Privacy violations
- Regulated product promotion (where prohibited)

**Commercial abuse**:
- Unsolicited advertising or promotions
- Pyramid schemes or MLM recruitment
- Competitor disparagement
- Unauthorized commercial solicitation

## Content Sharing Guidelines

### What You Can Share

- Personal experiences with our products/services
- Helpful tips, feedback, and suggestions
- Questions and requests for assistance
- User-generated content (following our UGC guidelines)
- Relevant industry news and insights

### When Sharing Our Content

- Credit {Brand Name} as the source
- Don't alter or misrepresent original content
- Provide context when sharing
- Follow platform-specific sharing guidelines

### Third-Party Content

- Only share content you have the right to use
- Credit original creators appropriately
- Respect copyright and intellectual property
- Verify accuracy before sharing

## Privacy & Personal Information

### Protect Your Privacy

- Don't share personal information publicly (address, phone, etc.)
- Be cautious about what you reveal in posts and comments
- Review your privacy settings regularly
- Report any suspicious requests for personal information

### Respect Others' Privacy

- Don't share others' personal information without consent
- Blur or obscure faces in photos when appropriate
- Ask permission before tagging others
- Respect requests to remove shared content

For information on how we handle your data, please review our [Privacy Policy]({URL}).
