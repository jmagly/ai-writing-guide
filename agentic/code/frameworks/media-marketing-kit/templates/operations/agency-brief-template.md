### For Campaign Manager

- Provide comprehensive context - more is better than less
- Be clear about must-have's vs. nice-to-have's
- Set realistic timelines with buffer for approvals
- Establish clear approval authority to avoid bottlenecks
- Maintain regular communication throughout project
- Document all decisions and changes in writing

### For Brand Manager

- Ensure brand guidelines are comprehensive and accessible
- Provide examples of good/bad brand execution
- Be available for brand questions throughout project
- Review creative early and often (not just final approval)
- Balance brand consistency with creative freedom

### For Legal/Compliance

- Review brief for legal/compliance requirements upfront
- Clarify what requires legal review (creative claims, competitive comparisons, etc.)
- Provide fast turnaround on legal reviews to avoid delays
- Educate agency on legal guardrails early in process

### For Finance/Procurement

- Ensure SOW and budget align with company policies
- Issue PO promptly after contract signing
- Track milestones and payments
- Monitor scope changes for budget impact
