## Agent Notes

### For Content Writer

- Ensure content serves audience first, promotion second
- Research thoroughly - cite credible sources
- Write for scanability (headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
- Integrate keywords naturally, never force
- Include clear, compelling CTAs
- Proofread meticulously before submitting for review
- Document research and sources for fact-checking

### For Content Manager

- Review against brief and campaign objectives
- Ensure consistent voice across all campaign content
- Validate claims and competitive statements
- Check that content delivers promised value to audience
- Confirm SEO best practices followed
- Coordinate with reviewers for timely approvals

### For SEO Specialist

- Validate keyword research and targeting
- Ensure technical SEO elements configured correctly
- Review internal linking strategy
- Check for featured snippet opportunities
- Monitor search performance post-publish
- Recommend optimizations based on ranking data

### For Brand Manager

- Verify tone and voice align with brand guidelines
- Check visual assets for brand consistency
- Ensure messaging aligns with brand positioning
- Flag any off-brand language or claims
- Approve before publish if brand-sensitive content

### For Campaign Manager

- Ensure content aligns with campaign timeline
- Coordinate content with other campaign assets
- Track content performance against campaign KPIs
- Identify high-performing content for additional promotion
- Document learnings for future campaigns
