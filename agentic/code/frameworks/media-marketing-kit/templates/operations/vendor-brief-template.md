## Agent Notes

### For Campaign Manager

- Ensure brief is comprehensive and leaves no ambiguity
- Provide all necessary brand assets and resources upfront
- Set clear expectations for timeline, revisions, and approvals
- Maintain regular communication with vendor throughout
- Document all decisions and changes
- Conduct post-project review to capture learnings

### For Brand Manager

- Review brief to ensure brand guidelines are clearly communicated
- Provide brand assets in all necessary formats
- Be available for brand-related questions during project
- Review deliverables for brand compliance
- Provide clear, actionable feedback during revisions

### For Legal/Compliance

- Review brief for legal/compliance requirements
- Ensure contract/terms cover all necessary protections
- Clarify usage rights and licensing upfront
- Review deliverables for legal compliance before approval
- Flag any regulatory risks early in process

### For Procurement/Finance

- Verify budget and payment terms align with company policy
- Issue purchase order if required
- Track invoices and payment schedule
- Ensure vendor meets insurance/documentation requirements
- Monitor scope changes for budget impact
