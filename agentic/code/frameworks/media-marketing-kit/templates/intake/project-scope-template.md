## Agent Notes

### For Project Manager

- **Scope Precision**: Ensure scope is specific enough to manage but flexible enough to adapt
- **Dependency Mapping**: Identify all dependencies early and track status rigorously
- **Risk Proactivity**: Focus on top 3-5 risks with active mitigation, not just monitoring
- **Communication Cadence**: Establish clear, consistent communication rhythms from day 1

### For Intake Coordinator

- **Completeness Verification**: Confirm all sections are filled with actionable detail
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Validate that all key stakeholders are identified and engaged
- **Approval Path**: Ensure approval process is clear and all approvers are available
- **Dependency Flags**: Surface any dependencies that could derail timeline early

### For Resource Manager

- **Capacity Planning**: Verify resource allocations are realistic given team capacity
- **Skill Matching**: Ensure people assigned have the skills for their responsibilities
- **Contention Management**: Flag any resource conflicts with other projects
- **Vendor Readiness**: Confirm external resources are contracted and ready to engage

### For Finance/Budget Owner

- **Budget Adequacy**: Validate budget is sufficient for stated scope
- **Category Allocation**: Ensure budget breakdown aligns with deliverables and timeline
- **Tracking Setup**: Confirm budget tracking process is in place before spend starts
- **Contingency Appropriateness**: Verify contingency % is appropriate for project risk

### For Campaign Strategist

- **Strategic Alignment**: Ensure project scope supports campaign objectives
- **Deliverable Relevance**: Confirm deliverables map to audience and channel strategy
- **Timeline Realism**: Validate timeline allows for quality creative and approvals
- **Integration Opportunities**: Identify ways to amplify impact through integration
