## Agent Notes

### For Brand Manager

- **Consistency Check**: Ensure all sections align and reinforce the same brand identity
- **Completeness**: Verify all visual assets are linked and accessible
- **Clarity**: Ensure voice/tone guidelines are specific enough to be actionable
- **Approval Status**: Confirm this brief has necessary stakeholder approval

### For Content Strategist

- **Message Testing**: Validate that messaging hierarchy reflects customer research
- **Tone Application**: Ensure tone guidelines can be practically applied across channels
- **Audience Mapping**: Verify messaging aligns with audience needs and preferences

### For Creative Director

- **Visual Integrity**: Ensure visual guidelines are comprehensive enough for creative execution
- **Flexibility**: Balance brand consistency with creative flexibility
- **Asset Availability**: Confirm all referenced visual assets are production-ready

### For Copywriter

- **Voice Clarity**: Ensure voice pillars are distinct and provide clear writing direction
- **Example Quality**: Verify examples accurately demonstrate intended voice/tone
- **Language Guidance**: Check that word preferences and grammar guidelines are comprehensive

### For Marketing Ops

- **Template Deployment**: Ensure templates are available in all necessary tools/platforms
- **Asset Distribution**: Verify brand assets are accessible to all who need them
- **Approval Workflow**: Confirm approval process is documented in workflow tools
